My Background

Behind Every Great NHS Application Is a Clear Story. Let's Find Yours.

With over a decade in the NHS and public sector, I've supported data, transformation, and workforce programmes that span health boards and digital teams across Wales. During that time, I've sat on interview panels for both clinical and non-clinical roles, from entry-level admin to senior digital leadership, giving me a clear understanding of what stands out in real shortlisting and interview decisions.

I've helped colleagues move confidently from Band 4 to Band 6, guided senior candidates into strategic digital roles, and supported applicants from overseas who needed help understanding NHS values and expectations.
