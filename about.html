Our Mission

Helping Health & Care Professionals Build Strong Applications

At Health Career Boost, our mission is simple: to help health and care professionals build strong applications, gain confidence, and secure the roles they deserve.

We support individuals applying to UK healthcare organisations, including the Health, private hospitals, care homes, community services, and public-sector teams. Whether you're taking your first step into health and social care or aiming for a Band 7 or Band 8a role, we provide the expert guidance you need to stand out.

Who We Are

Bridging the Gap Between Talent and Opportunity

Health Career Boost was created to bridge a real gap in healthcare recruitment.

Many talented professionals struggle to showcase their skills on paper — not because they lack ability, but because the application and interview processes can be confusing, competitive, and full of hidden expectations.

Our coaching approach is grounded in:

  • Real experience supporting UK health and care applicants
  • Deep understanding of job requirements, competency frameworks, and values-based recruitment
  • Proven strategies that align with what hiring managers actually look for

We don't offer generic advice — we offer clarity, structure, and personalised support that leads to real progress.

What We Do

Strengthening Every Part of Your Application Journey

We help you strengthen every part of your application journey

CV Review & Optimisation

Transform your CV into a clear, impact-driven document tailored to the role and band you want.

Supporting Statement Coaching

Learn how to write structured STAR examples that demonstrate your value, experience, and alignment with role expectations.

Mock Interview Coaching

Build confidence with realistic interview practice, values-based questions, feedback, and techniques to communicate clearly and professionally.

Career Boost Package

A complete job application upgrade including CV, supporting statement, interviewing skill development, and 30 days of messaging support.
